Steps

  1. Boil the Meat: In a pot, submerge the pork belly and pig face in water with salt and a bay leaf. Simmer for 40 to 60 minutes until tender.
  2. Grill and Chop: Drain the meat and let it cool. Grill over an open flame or in a grilling pan until the edges are charred and slightly crispy. In a separate pan, cook and mash the chicken liver. Chop all grilled meats and the liver into fine, small pieces.
  3. Sauté and Combine: In a large pan, heat oil and a bit of butter. Sauté the minced onions until soft, then add the chopped meat and mashed chicken liver.
  4. Season: Stir in the soy sauce, chili peppers, and black pepper. Turn off the heat and fold in the mayonnaise and fresh calamansi juice.
  5. Serve Sizzling: Heat a cast-iron sizzling plate on the stove, add a teaspoon of butter. Transfer the sisig to the sizzling plate. Top with crushed chicharon for crunch and an optional raw egg to mix in while it is hot.
